Features

Secure

Every transaction is secured with robust cryptography, distributed though the global peer-to-peer consensus network.

Private

Bitnote utilizes the cryptographic system to transfer funds without identifying information of each user becoming visible within the blockchain.

Untraceable

Bitnote uses ring signatures to make transactions untraceable, meaning its nearly impossible to use blockchain analysis to determine if funds have been spent.

Limited Supply

No more than 12 million Bitnote will ever exist within the blockchain.

Fast Syncing

Bitnote's peer-to-peer network allows for substantially fast blockchain syncing compared to that of Bitcoin.

ASIC Resistant

Bitnote utilizes the CryptoNight-Dark (0.5 MIB ScratchPad) PoW hashing algorithm, making it resistant to ASIC based machines.

Low Fees

Bitnote allows anyone to send fast, private transactions anywhere, to anyone around the world for nearly zero-fees.

F.A.Q

Why should I invest in Bitnote?

Bitnote seeks to provide fast, private and untraceable transactions to anyone, anywhere around the world. Bitnote is to be a trusted, stable, long-term, store-of-value for your money.

What’s the long term plan for Bitnote?

Bitnote is still a very experimental Cryptocurrency based on the CryptoNote technology, and is being improved daily by its developers. If you're a developer and are wanting to help contribute to the Bitnote codebase, checkout our Github Page for more info on contributing.

Where can I read your whitepapers?

Bitnote is a fork of CryptoNote implementation, we've felt it was unnecessary at this time.

What does the team intend to do with premined coins?

Bitnote held a pre-mine of 1 million coin. This may seem like a lot, however factoring in costs such as but not limited to, exchange listing fees (usually 1 - 15 Bitcoin), seed node hosting, website domains, community bounties, advertising, will be covered by the pre-mine fund to ensure Bitnote runs fast and efficently for the years to come...
